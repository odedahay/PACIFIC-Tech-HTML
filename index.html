About Us

Research, Training, Innovation, and Collaboration for Community Engagement, Sustainable and Safe Environment Incorporated (PACIFICTECH RTIC CESSE INC) or PACIFICTECH is a Philippine Non-Government Organization (NGO) research, training, innovations, and collaboration for community engagement, sustainable and safe environment with programs in research methodologies, research publishing, research documents retrieval, and related activities.
